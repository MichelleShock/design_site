About Me

I have a background in graphic design but have spent the last 10 years developing a love for technology working for an ISP. Creative problem solving is my forte and I'm always up for a challenge.

In my spare time I enjoy time with my family and friends. I love trying new things and gaining different perspectives. Over the years I've tried my hand at welding, playing the musical saw, cycling, knitting, and numerous diy projects. I'm an unapologetic fan of puns and have an affinity for the absurd.
